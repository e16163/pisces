The Process

Three steps to instant results

01

Take a Selfie

Open Pisces in your browser and take a clear, well-lit selfie. No app download, no account setup — just you and a camera.

02

AI Analyzes Your Features

Our model reads over 200 facial biometric data points — bone structure, facial volume, proportions and more — to build a precise body composition estimate.

03

Track Your Progress

See your estimated weight, BMI range, and health insights. Log daily scans to visualize your journey — all right in the browser, no equipment required.

What Sets Us Apart

Scale-Free Measurement

Whether you're traveling, at the gym, or just at home without a scale, Pisces is always ready to give you an instant reading from any device with a camera.

Daily Progress Tracking

Build a visual timeline of your weight journey. Charts and trend lines make it easy to see patterns and stay motivated over weeks and months.

Privacy First

All analysis happens on-device. Your photos are never stored or transmitted. Your face — and your health data — belongs only to you.
